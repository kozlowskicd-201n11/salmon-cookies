About Us!

Welcome to the official homepage for Seattle's original Northwest snack, salmon cookies! We have been producing and distributing salmon cookies locally around the Seattle Metro area since last week, and in that time we have seen a huge increase in demand. We have to date opened a total of five locations to serve everyone's salmon cookie needs.

Salmon cookies are a healthy snack for the whole family! As their name suggests, our salmon cookies are produced with locally raised Chinook salmon as their number three ingredient, right behind sugar and butter. There has never, in the history of all time, been a single instance of someone not enjoying a salmon cookie. Share them far and wide with loved ones and not-so-loved ones alike, and receive the adulation of all!

We have more to offer than delectibal treats, however! We have fully capitalized on the salmon cookie sensation to bring you a huge line of merchandise through our online store, including cookie cutters, fridge magnates, pipe wrenches, longboards, lawn ornaments, pet chew toys, and our exclusive clothing line by Giorgio Armani. Bulk purchases of cookies are also available for international shipping, so you can enjoy salmon cookies anytime, anywhere!
